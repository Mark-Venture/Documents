# How-to documents for DroidLink

* [How to create sequences in DroidLink](https://github.com/Mark-Venture/Documents/blob/main/DroidLink_Sequences_How-To.md) - This walks you through creating a sequence of lights, sounds and servo movements in DroidLink
* [Electronics_Needed_for_R2.md](https://github.com/Mark-Venture/Documents/blob/main/Electronics_Needed_for_R2.md) - A list of the electronics needed for R2D2 and Droidlink - It includes parts, links, and details
* [Sharing Maestro Sequences](https://github.com/Mark-Venture/Documents/blob/main/INPROGRESS_-_Sharing_Maestro_Sequences_-_The_Python_Way.md) - A way of exporting and sharing your settings.txt with other users, or adjusting their exported settings for your droid.  This is also helpful if you have to change a servo.
* [PWM to SBUS-A Translator](https://github.com/Mark-Venture/Documents/blob/main/PWM_to_SBUSA_translator/ReadMe.MD) -  If you have an ESP32 and breakout board, and can't get your hands on a HotRC SBUS-A receiver,  this is a temporary work-around until you get a real SBUS-A receiver.
* [PoorMans Magic Panel](https://github.com/Mark-Venture/Documents/tree/main/PoorMans_Magic_Panel)  - a simple and cheap Magic Panel led setup for your Astromech.  While not as nice as the one from Printed-Droid.com, or West_Worx,  its very cheap to make and gives you many of the patters and color options.
